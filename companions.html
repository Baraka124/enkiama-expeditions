Companions

Some journeys are composed for one group.
Others open a few seats to the right company.

For the traveller who wants Tanzania, and the right people to share it with. We compose the company as carefully as we compose the journey.

Not a group tour. A small circle of people who would have chosen each other, had they met first.

Open journeys

A few composed journeys, with room for the right people.

Now and then we compose a journey with a particular character — a season, a pace, a way of seeing — and open two or three seats. Because the journey’s spirit is set, the people who join tend to belong together. These are rare, and small, and never a catalogue.

The journeys below show the kind we open. When a season is live, its seats are real — write to us and we will tell you what is open now.

Journeys seeking companions

Composing a journey, and open to company?

If you are planning a journey of your own and would welcome the right people alongside you, tell us. We hold these quietly, and introduce travellers only where we sense a genuine fit — in temperament, in pace, in what pulls them to Tanzania. You are never posted to a board. You are considered, by a person, with care.

Tell us a little about the journey you imagine, and the kind of company that would make it richer.

Baraka reads every message personally. We introduce people only where the fit feels real — and speak with each traveller first. Nothing is ever automated, and no one is ever posted publicly.

Your journey is with us.

We will sit with it, and write back — whether that is an introduction, an open journey that might suit you, or simply the beginning of composing your own.

i

We match on character, not convenience

Temperament, pace, and what draws someone to Tanzania matter more than dates. The company is composed, not assembled.

ii

A person, never a platform

Every introduction is made by hand, after a conversation with each traveller. There is no board, no listing, no algorithm.

iii

You are always free to say no

An introduction is only ever an introduction. If the fit is not right for you, it does not happen. Your own journey remains yours.

Whether you travel alone, as two, or as a circle still forming — the journey begins the same way.
