# Enkiama — Photo Naming Guide

Drop your photos into this folder (`assets/images/`) using the EXACT filenames
below. The website picks them up automatically. Any photo you haven't added yet
shows a tinted placeholder instead — nothing breaks, so you can fill them in over
time.

## Rules
- Use the exact filename (lowercase, .jpg). `serengeti.jpg`, not `Serengeti.JPG`.
- Landscape orientation for all except the guide portraits (those can be portrait).
- Aim for ~2000px on the long edge, good light, no heavy filters.
- These are hero images — choose your strongest, most evocative shots.

## After you add photos
Commit them with the rest of the site and push. They'll be live in a minute.
Because they load from your own domain, they're fast and they get indexed by
Google Image search (a real traffic source for safari). Keep filenames descriptive
— that IS the SEO.
