Before
you write.

Three questions we are asked before almost every first conversation — answered here so you arrive without hesitation.

01

Do I need to know what I want?

No. Most of the people who write to us have a feeling, not a plan — a sense that Tanzania is pulling at them without knowing exactly which world, which season, which kind of experience. That is the right starting point. Our first conversation is not a briefing; it is a discovery. We ask questions. We listen. The journey takes shape from there.

02

Is there a minimum budget?

We do not publish prices, because we do not have products to price. Your budget is not a hurdle to clear — it is the cloth the journey is cut from. Tell us what this is worth to you, and we compose the most extraordinary thing that figure can hold. Different budgets compose into genuinely different journeys, each one whole, none a stripped version of another. We are honest, early, when a figure cannot hold what you are imagining.

03

How far in advance?

The earlier the better — not because of a season, but because composing something properly takes time. Tanzania rewards travel in every month of the year, and the right window depends entirely on what you want to see and feel. The migration, the calving, the green season, the dry: each is its own country. Tell us when you are thinking, and we will tell you honestly what that time of year makes possible, and how soon we would need to begin.

After

What happens next

From first message
to Tanzania.

01

Conversation

We talk — or write

A free-form exchange: who you are, who is travelling, what you dream about. No form, no template. We follow your lead on pace and format.

02

Assembly

We think, consult, and build

We speak with our partners. We consider the season. We begin assembling a journey — not from a catalogue, but from actual people and places we know and trust.

03

Proposal

You receive a full proposal

A complete document: day-by-day journey, named partners, cost breakdown with no hidden elements. Everything you need to make a decision — and everything you can question.

04

Refinement

We refine until it is right

Most proposals go through two or three rounds of adjustment. The journey belongs to you — we keep working until every element feels right, then confirm with partners and secure your dates.

Our commitments

What you can
expect from us
always.

These are not terms and conditions. They are the way we work — because we believe the experience of planning a journey should feel like the journey itself: warm, clear, and without unpleasant surprises.

We respond to every message, usually the same day

We never send a price list without having spoken first

The number we give you is the number — no top-ups, no surprises

We tell you when something is not right for your group — including when that means suggesting a different operator who can do it better

We take few groups at a time, so each one has our full attention

Year-round — every season in Tanzania has something the others do not

Partners selected for what they know, who they are, and how they are with people — not for availability or margin

We remain reachable throughout your journey, not just before it

When a village has a local guide whose knowledge exceeds ours, we bring them in — by name, at a fair fee, transparently

When another operator can do one thing better than we can, we say so and involve them — we are building the best possible journey, not protecting a monopoly

Not a tour. An experience built only for you — kwa moyo wote.
